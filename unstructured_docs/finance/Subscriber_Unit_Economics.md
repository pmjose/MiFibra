# Customer Unit Economics Analysis
**MiFibra Communications - LTV/CAC Analysis**

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines customer lifetime value (LTV), customer acquisition cost (CAC), and payback metrics across MiFibra's customer segments. The blended LTV:CAC ratio of 6.2x demonstrates strong unit economics with an average payback period of 14 months.
