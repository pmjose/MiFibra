# Campaign ROI Analysis 2024
**MiFibra Communications - Marketing Effectiveness Report**

## Executive Summary

Full year 2024 marketing investment of **S/18 million** generated **25,500 MQLs** at a blended cost per lead of **S/706**, delivering an estimated **S/210 million in pipeline** and a marketing ROI of **11.7x**. This report analyses campaign performance by type, channel, and segment to optimise 2025 investment allocation.
