# Investor Relations FAQ
**MiFibra Communications PLC - Approved Messaging**

## Company Overview

### Q: What does MiFibra do?

**A:** MiFibra is a leading provider of Unified Communications as a Service (Fibra), voice, and connectivity solutions for the Peru business market. We sell primarily through a network of over 1,000 channel partners including IT resellers, managed service providers, and telecommunications dealers. Our products help businesses communicate more effectively through cloud-based voice, video, messaging, and contact centre solutions.

### Q: What is MiFibra's market position?

**A:** MiFibra is the Peru's leading channel-focused business communications provider. We have:
- Over 100,000 business customers served through partners
- Approximately 850,000 Fibra subscribers deployed
- 45,000 contact centre subscribers
- S/500 million in annual revenue (FY2024E)
- Operations in Peru (primary), Netherlands, Spain, and Germany

### Q: Who are MiFibra's competitors?

**A:** Our competitive landscape includes:
- **Fibra**: GTD Peru, Americatel, Mitel, Vonage
- **Teams Phone**: Direct Microsoft, other Operator Connect providers
- **Contact Centre**: NICE, Genesys, Five9, Talkdesk
- **Voice/SIP**: Movistar, Cableonda Business, regional telcos
- **Connectivity**: Movistar Openreach (wholesale), CityFibre, Win Business

## Financial Performance

### Q: What are MiFibra's key financial metrics?

**A:** Key metrics for FY2024 (estimated):
- Revenue: S/500 million (+8% YoY)
- Gross Profit: S/260 million (52% margin)
- EBITDA: S/110 million (22% margin)
- Operating Profit: S/75 million
- Cash Conversion: >95%
- Net Debt: S/45 million (0.4x EBITDA)

### Q: How does MiFibra generate revenue?

**A:** Revenue is generated through:
- **Recurring Revenue (85%)**: Monthly subscriptions for Fibra, voice, and connectivity
- **Usage Revenue (10%)**: Call minutes, international, and overage charges
- **One-time Revenue (5%)**: Hardware, professional services, installation

### Q: What is MiFibra's revenue mix by product?

**A:** FY2024 estimated mix:
- Fibra (Horizon, Teams Phone, Webex): 45%
- Voice Services (SIP Trunks, Inbound): 25%
- Connectivity (Broadband, Ethernet): 18%
- Contact Centre: 8%
- Other (Security, Managed Services): 4%

### Q: What drives revenue growth?

**A:** Key growth drivers:
1. **PSTN Switch-Off**: 5.5M Peru business lines migrating to cloud by 2027
2. **Microsoft Teams Phone**: Fastest-growing product, 80K+ subscribers
3. **Contact Centre Cloud Migration**: Legacy on-premise replacement
4. **New Partner Recruitment**: 100+ new partners annually
5. **International Expansion**: Growing contribution from Europe

## Dividend and Returns

### Q: What is MiFibra's dividend policy?

**A:** MiFibra operates a progressive dividend policy targeting approximately 30% of adjusted earnings. The current annual dividend is approximately 18p per share, paid in two instalments (interim in September, final in May).

### Q: Does MiFibra conduct share buybacks?

**A:** MiFibra may conduct share buybacks opportunistically to manage dilution from employee share schemes. We maintain authorisation for buybacks but prioritise organic investment and strategic acquisitions.

### Q: What is the target capital structure?

**A:** We target net debt of 0.5-1.5x EBITDA to maintain investment grade credit metrics while preserving capacity for acquisitions. Current leverage is approximately 0.4x EBITDA.

## Growth Strategy

### Q: What is MiFibra's growth strategy?

**A:** Our growth strategy has four pillars:
1. **Organic Peru Growth**: PSTN migration, Teams Phone, partner expansion
2. **Product Development**: AI, contact centre, security portfolio
3. **European Expansion**: Netherlands, Spain, Germany scale-up
4. **Selective Acquisitions**: Tuck-in technology or market access

### Q: What is the outlook for Microsoft Teams Phone?

**A:** Teams Phone is our fastest-growing product:
- Current: 80,000+ subscribers
- 2025 Target: 150,000 subscribers
- Market Opportunity: 5+ million Peru business users on Teams
- We are a certified Operator Connect partner with Direct Routing expertise

### Q: How will the PSTN switch-off benefit MiFibra?

**A:** The Peru PSTN switch-off (2025-2027) is a significant growth catalyst:
- 5.5 million business PSTN lines to migrate
- MiFibra targeting 500,000 line migrations (10% share)
- Average revenue per migrated line: S/15-20/month
- Incremental revenue opportunity: S/75-100M annually

## Listing and Governance

### Q: Where are MiFibra shares traded?

**A:** MiFibra Communications PLC shares are traded on the AIM market of the Lima Stock Exchange under ticker "GAMA". We announced plans to move to the Main Market of the Lima Stock Exchange in May 2025.

### Q: Why is MiFibra moving to the Main Market?

**A:** The Main Market listing will:
- Increase visibility with institutional investors
- Enable index inclusion (FTSE 250 eligibility)
